Cream of broccoli

mushed up broccoli

sweated veggies with a light roux

followed by a cook on the broccoli and the blending with cream

    Ingredients
  • Shallots
  • leeks
  • broccoli
  • butter
  • cream
  • stock
  • celery
    Steps
  1. Sweat all but broccoli
  2. Add flour
  3. Add stock and bring to boil
  4. add broccoli and cook until soft
  5. blend then add cream
  6. bring to boil for a second then season
